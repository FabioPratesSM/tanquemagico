Usage Instructions

For Washing:

1. Choose the appropriate washing machine (8kg, 12kg, or 20kg) and place your clothes inside.

2. Make the payment at the central unit and select the number of your washing machine.

3. Choose the washing temperature on the washing machine by pressing the respective arrows.

4. To start the washing program, press the "Start" button.

For Drying:

1. Choose one of the dryers and place your clothes inside.

2. Make the payment at the central unit and select the number of your dryer.

3. Choose one of the drying temperatures on the dryer.

4. To start the drying program, press the "Start" button.
